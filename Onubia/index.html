About Onubia

Onubia is a blockchain-powered online marketplace for indigenous African products with a native coin on the Binance Smart-Chain.
We promote native African products, helping them gain worldwide recognition and bringing their products to the global market.
Our online marketplace comes with the Nubia Token, its native cryptocurrency, through which users can make payment on the platform.
